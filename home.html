So how does a outdoorsmen find his way into a career behind a screen?

Growing up, I always loved building things. Whether just Lego Sets, or duct tape wallets, or cubes out of grocery store twisty-ties, I was always building. My magnum-opus was a ping pong ball dispenser made out of an old cereal box. This love for building was always there, but as I went through school I started to lose touch with it more as it was less present in my life. Luckily, I decided to take in a Computer Science course in highschool, which allowed me to jump back and reconnect with my inner child, switching from the physical world to the digital world. Because of this class, I enrolled in a Intro Computer SCience course, where I learned the basics of programming and got to build a game through PyGame. This class helped me confirm Computer Science was the route I wanted to go down. Now, I'm trying to turn my love of coding into a professional career.
